Register your Battle tag in the bot.

A Battle tag is a combination of a <i>Display Name</i> and a <i>Code</i> that identifies a user in Blizzard games. It looks like:
<code>MyName#12345</code>

You can check your Battle tag in the Battle Net app, near the top right corner.
To send me your Battle tag you can use:
<code>/register [battletag]</code>

<b>Example</b>:
<code>/register MyName#12345</code>
